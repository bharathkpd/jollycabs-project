Comfortable, safe, and affordable cab packages for the sacred Srisailam pilgrimage.

Home Srisailam Tour

One of South India's Most Sacred Destinations

Srisailam is home to the Mallikarjuna Jyotirlinga, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas in India. Located approximately 230 km from Hyderabad, it is one of the most visited pilgrimage sites in Andhra Pradesh.

Our Srisailam cab packages include a dedicated driver who is familiar with the route, all tolls covered in the fare, and vehicles that are maintained for long-distance comfort.

Itinerary

Standard Day Trip Timeline

05:00

Departure from Hyderabad

Pick up from your doorstep in Hyderabad or Secunderabad. Start early to avoid city traffic.

10:00

Arrive Srisailam, Temple Darshan

Reach the sacred temple hill and head for Mallikarjuna Jyotirlinga and Bhramaramba Devi darshan.

13:00

Lunch Break at Srisailam

Delectable vegetarian lunch break at the local temple dining halls or surrounding restaurants.

14:30

Visit Dam & Akhanda Jyothi

Explore the engineering marvel of Srisailam Dam and visit the nearby Akhanda Jyothi Mandir shrine.

17:00

Departure from Srisailam

Start the return journey back toward Hyderabad, crossing the forest checkpost before sunset.

20:00

Arrive Hyderabad

Reach Hyderabad and get dropped off directly back at your home or hotel. Tour ends.
